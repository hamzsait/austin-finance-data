Pro-Israel Money in Austin City Council

Tracking Austin campaign donors who also donated to AIPAC PAC, United Democracy Project, and other pro-Israel committees at the federal level. This page shows how much these donors gave locally to each current council member.

Important: The dollar amounts on this page represent local Austin campaign contributions from individual donors who also donated to pro-Israel political committees at the federal level. This does not mean any council member received money directly from AIPAC or any pro-Israel organization. We are mapping the overlap between local donor networks and federal pro-Israel political spending.

Methodology

This analysis cross-references two datasets:

  • Local data: Austin campaign finance filings from the City of Austin, covering all individual and PAC contributions to council member campaigns. Rows marked as corrections (correction = "X") are excluded to avoid double-counting.
  • Federal data: FEC individual contribution records matched to Austin donors. We identify donors who contributed to committees classified as pro-Israel, including:
    • AIPAC-aligned / Hawkish: AIPAC PAC, United Democracy Project (AIPAC's Super PAC), Pro-Israel America PAC, NORPAC, and similar committees.
    • Liberal Zionist: J Street PAC and affiliated committees.
  • "Pro-Israel money" on this page means: money given locally to Austin candidates by donors who have a demonstrated pattern of donating to pro-Israel committees at the federal level. It does NOT mean money sent by AIPAC or any pro-Israel committee to these candidates.
  • Donor matching between local and federal records uses name, ZIP code, and employer data with confidence scoring.
